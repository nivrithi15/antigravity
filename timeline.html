Voxara Timeline

Click on any stage to learn more about the process.

Announcement of Elections

The official election body announces the dates and schedule for the upcoming elections. This marks the formal start of the election period.

Candidate Nominations

Individuals who wish to run for office submit their formal nomination papers. These are scrutinized to ensure candidates meet all eligibility criteria.

Campaigning

Candidates share their platforms, hold rallies, debate, and advertise to convince citizens to vote for them. This period ends shortly before voting begins.

Voting Day

Citizens go to polling stations or mail in their ballots to cast their votes. This is the core democratic action of the entire process.

Vote Counting

Once polls close, officials securely tally all valid votes. This process requires strict oversight to ensure accuracy and transparency.

Results Declaration

The final tallies are confirmed, and the winning candidates are officially declared. They will then prepare to take office.
